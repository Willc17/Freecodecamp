Gabriel "FalleN" Toledo de Alcântara Sguario

The best Awper player in the history of CS:GO

The Story of Counter-Strike Career of FalleN:

  • Gabriel Toledo de Alcântara Sguario born in May 30, 1991.
  • Professionally known by his in-game name, FalleN is a Brazilian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player and former Counter-Strike: Source and Counter-Strike 1.6 player.
  • 2015 - he was chosen the most influential person Brazilian's eSports.
  • 2016 - He was also nominated PC personality of the year by the eSports Industry Awards. He is the owner of Brazilian eSports organization Games Academy.
  • During the years 2016 and 2017, he was described as the best Awper, in-game leader, overall player and one of the few players who are awper in-game leader, making his achievements even more exciting.
